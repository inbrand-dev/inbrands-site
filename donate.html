Built by a solo developer

InTrack is built as a practical, local-first habit and behavior tracking app for Android.

If InTrack helps you stay consistent, track your progress, or better understand your routines, you can support continued development with a voluntary donation.

Donations help cover development time, testing devices, app store costs, and future improvements.

No ads. No data selling. No account required.

InTrack is designed to keep user tracking data local to the device. It does not use analytics, advertising, third-party tracking SDKs, or cloud accounts.
