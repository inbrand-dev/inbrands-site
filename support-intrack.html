Built independently

InTrack is a publicly released Android habit tracker built and maintained independently. It is designed for private, flexible, real-life habit tracking without accounts, ads, analytics, or cloud tracking.

If InTrack helps you stay consistent, track your progress, or better understand your routines, the best ways to support it are to use the app, share it with someone who might benefit, leave an honest Google Play review, send useful feedback, or consider InTrack Plus if the extra features are useful to you.

Best ways to help

  • Download and use InTrack from Google Play.
  • Leave an honest review if the app has been useful.
  • Share InTrack with someone who needs a private or flexible habit tracker.
  • Send bug reports, feature feedback, or screenshots when something is unclear.
  • Consider InTrack Plus if the optional extra features are useful to you.

No ads. No data selling. No account required.

InTrack is designed to keep habit and tracking data local to the device. It does not use analytics, advertising, third-party tracking SDKs, or cloud accounts.

Support helps keep development focused on useful tracking features instead of ads or data collection.

Direct donations

Direct donation links are not currently available on this site. For now, the simplest way to support continued development is through Google Play reviews, feedback, sharing, and optional InTrack Plus features.

External donation options may be considered later if there is a clear need for them.
