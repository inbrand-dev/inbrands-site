When reporting a bug

To make bug reports easier to investigate, include as much of the following as possible:

  • App name: InTrack
  • App version, if visible in the app or Google Play
  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • Your Android version
  • Your phone or tablet model
  • Whether the issue happens every time or only sometimes
  • Screenshots, if useful and safe to share

Privacy requests

InTrack stores habit and tracking data locally on the device. It does not use accounts, cloud sync, analytics, ads, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Because InTrack does not transmit user habit or tracking data to InBrands, most user data can be deleted directly by deleting data inside the app, clearing app storage in Android settings, or uninstalling the app.
