InTrack

InTrack is a private offline habit tracker for Android built around real-life routines, flexible schedules, custom day boundaries, groups, widgets, reminders, stats, and no-account tracking.

InTrack is available on Google Play and is actively maintained.

  • Fixed Today card period totals for weekly and interval-based trackers.

Development focus

InTrack is continuing to improve around flexible scheduling, local-first privacy, widgets, groups, statistics, and tracker types that fit real routines instead of forcing every habit into the same daily checkbox.

Planned and in-progress work may change as features are tested and refined.

Earlier foundation

  • Core tracker creation and logging.
  • Calendar history and summary views.
  • Streaks, completion rates, and progress statistics.
  • Local reminders using Android notifications.
  • Local-first data storage.
  • Export and import backup support.
  • Optional local PIN lock protection.
